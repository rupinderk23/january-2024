Mercedes-Benz Awards Night: An Ode to Champions

GSK Annual Business Managers’ Meet 2025

Colonel Lalit Rai

Neeti Mohan

The GSK Annual Business Managers’ Meet 2025 in Hyderabad brought together field leaders as GSK entered its second century in India, focusing on priorities for the year ahead. Discussions centered on the importance of adapting to change, maintaining discipline, and the role of leadership in driving alignment toward shared goals.

The event featured a keynote by Colonel Lalit Rai, who shared valuable insights from his time in the armed forces. His keynote addressed the importance of teamwork, resilience, and decision-making under pressure, offering key takeaways for leadership across industries.

The evening ended with a lively performance by Neeti Mohan, who had the crowd swaying to hits like Meri Jaan and Tune Maari Entriyaan. The combination of meaningful discussions and music created a well-rounded experience, leaving attendees with both inspiration and energy.

Hilti Kick-off 2025: A Celebration of Team Spirit and Innovation

Golden Girls

Malkeet Singh - CYR Wheel

Bad Salsa

Rajat Kumar - Sand Artist

Hilti Manufacturing India Pvt. Ltd. ushered in 2025 with a two-day event that celebrated the achievements of the past year and laid the foundation for the year ahead. The event was an occasion to recognize the hard work and dedication of the team, featuring performances that added energy to the celebration.

The evening featured Golden Girls, an all-women dance crew known for their energetic and synchronized performances. Their lively routines brought the stage to life, setting an upbeat tone for the celebration. Malkeet Singh, known for his impressive Cyr wheel skills, delivered a solo act that drew the audience in with his skillful and fluid performance. Bad Salsa, the acrobatic salsa duo that gained recognition from reality shows like India’s Got Talent and America’s Got Talent, impressed with their gravity-defying moves.

Rajat Kumar, an expressive performer known for his storytelling through sand art, closed the evening with an emotional act dedicated to Hilti’s commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI), which connected deeply with the team and reinforced the company’s core values.
