Talents in Spotlight - Women's Day Speakers
This month, as we gear up for the upcoming Women’s Day next month, we celebrate the voices of women who are shaping conversations and driving impact. Through their journeys and achievements, they are inspiring change and making a meaningful difference across diverse fields. Our Talent Spotlight features women who have broken barriers and set new benchmarks in sports, business, science, and more.
Sports

Manu Bhaker

Arjuna Award-winning shooter and Commonwealth Games gold medalist, redefining India's presence in global shooting sports.

Mithali Raj

Padma Shri and Khel Ratna awardee, India's cricketing legend and highest run-scorer in women's ODIs.

Business

Naina Lal Kidwai

Padma Shri awardee and the first Indian woman to graduate from Harvard Business School, shaping India's financial sector.

Radhika Gupta

Fortune India's "Most Powerful Women" leader, driving change as the CEO of Edelweiss Asset Management.

IT/Science

Anuradha TK

ISRO’s first woman satellite project director, leading India’s space communications revolution.

Susmita Mohanty

Aerospace entrepreneur and global thinker shaping the future of space exploration.

Bollywood

Zoya Akhtar

Award-winning filmmaker behind Gully Boy, India’s official Oscar entry, redefining Bollywood storytelling.

Sonali Bendre

Filmfare-winning actress and cancer survivor, inspiring millions with her journey of resilience and strength.
